About Us

Introduction

TK’s is in manufacturing and exports of Rock Salt products since 1999. After operating its manufacturing unit in Mirpur AJK till 2017, keeping in view the demand of growing salt market, TK’s shifted its manufacturing facility to Khushab (Punjab Province, Pakistan) in 2018 for better access to local salt mines so that quality raw material could be arranged as per requirements of international market of Himalayan Salt.

TK’s is exporting its products to a number of countries in Europe, North & South America particularly to Germany, United Kingdom and United States. TK’s is one the largest exports based manufacturing units of its kind which consists of a vast factory having skilled labour and efficient staff.

The Product

TK’s has been manufacturing numerous Rock Salt Products with different specifications and quantities as per requirement of international Rock Salt Market. The main products fall under basic categories namely Table Salt, Decorative Salt, Bath Salt and Animal Lick stones.

Skilled workers at TK’s use centuries old art of handcrafting Salt Lamps and Candleholders (T-Light Holders) in different shapes and designs. Most of the designs are conceived by the production team however, on demand, decorative salt products can be produced according to given designs and specs. Salt Lamps and T-Light Holders are not just abstract salt stones; instead each piece is crafted with hands which give it a unique and attractive shape.

Food Salt produced at TK’s gets tested on regular basis from recognized laboratories against specified standards. Each lot of food salt must pass lab test from NPSL or PCSIR Labs before export.

During the recent years, TK’s has not only enhanced its production capacity but also enhanced the quality of its product through strict quality control. From purchase of raw material to packing the finished products, whole process undergoes a number of quality checks at each stage. That’s why not only volume of exports has grown but TK’s has earned the trust among its international customers.

Registrations and Memberships

TK’s is registered with all government/semi-government departments which are required for any exporter. TK’s is registered with TDAP (formerly Export Promotion Bureau of Pakistan) and is a member of MCC&I, formerly AJKCCI (Azad Jammu & Kashmir Chamber of Commerce & Industries, Mirpur AJK). TK’s also bears license from Punjab Food Authority (for execution of Food Business). TK’s manufacturing facility is registered with FDA and bears BSCI certification as well. TK’s Natural Stone is also certified for Global HACCP for production and export of rock Salt products, ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management System and ISO 22000:2018 Food Safety Management System.

TK’s follows fair trade policy and child labor is strictly prohibited. Labor is provided with full rights as per local and national labor laws. TK’s is also registered with Punjab Employees Social Security Institution (PESSI) and Employees Old-Age Benefits Institution (EOBI).

The form of business is “Sole Proprietorship” and Mr. Tariq Mahmood Akhtar is Sole Owner of this business.
